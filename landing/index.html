Fetchwave bundles the best open-source tools into a single desktop experience.

Video & audio download YouTube, Vimeo, and hundreds of other sites via yt-dlp.
MP3 extraction Toggle "Audio Only" in the format picker. Real MP3 output, not renamed WebM.
Download queue Parallel downloads with progress, pause, resume, cancel, and automatic retries.
Built-in updater Auto-checks for updates. One click to install. No manual re-download.
Bundled yt-dlp + ffmpeg Ships inside the app. Nothing extra to install, configure, or maintain.
Subtitle support Download or embed subtitles in multiple languages automatically.
